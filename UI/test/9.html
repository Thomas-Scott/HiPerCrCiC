2012-13 All-Sports Schedule



Wainman is a third team D3football.com All-Region selection
WEDNESDAY, DEC 5, 2012 - MINNEAPOLIS - St. Olaf first year punter/kicker Lucas Wainman was named to the D3football.com All-West Region third team on Tuesday.

Women's basketball defeats St. Catherine for first MIAC win
WEDNESDAY, DEC 5, 2012 - NORTHFIELD, Minn.- Three Oles were in double figures as the St. Olaf women's basketball team picked up its first MIAC win of the year with a 72-38 win over St. Catherine on Wednesday night at the Skoglund Center.

Rosenthal is a third team all-region selection
WEDNESDAY, DEC 5, 2012 - KANSAS CITY, Kan. - St. Olaf junior David Rosenthal was named to the National Soccer Coaches Association of America All-North Region third team on Wednesday, the group announced.



Ole women skate to sweep
SUNDAY, DEC 2, 2012 - WINONA, Minn. - Nicole Stumpf had a goal and an assist as St. Olaf completed a two-game MIAC women's hockey sweep of Saint Mary's University on Saturday afternoon.

Cecka, Harris lead Oles to victory
SATURDAY, DEC 1, 2012 - NORTHFIELD, Minn. - Dan Cecka had two goals and two assists, while Jeff Harris had a goal and two assists as St. Olaf defeated Saint Mary's University 5-1 in a MIAC men's hockey game on Saturday night at Northfield Ice Arena.
